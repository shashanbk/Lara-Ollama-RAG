Performance Appraisal policy at your company:
• Performance appraisal is done on completion of a one-year service with
the company.
• Performance bonuses and/or increments are done based on the
performance appraisal report.
• An employee cannot switch jobs for six months after their appraisal.
• Performance appraisal is done based on the below KRAs:
• Technical
• Work output and/or Meeting project deadline.
• Work ethics and/or discipline.
• Peer review by team and Reporting manager.
• Quality of work.
• Meeting average working hours.

Leave Policy:
• The company will offer 16 leave every year. The leave has been divided into
different categories like Casual Leave, Privilege Leave, every year this leave
credit in part every three months. The bifurcation of all these leaves is given
below.
o Casual Leave (CL): 10 days
Applicable for personal reasons, unplanned short leaves, or emergencies.
and it will not be carried forward to next year.
o Privilege Leave (PL): 6 days
The employee may utilize this leave as intended, and if not utilized, the
remaining leave balance is carried forward each year. In the event that an
employee joins the company and does not avail of PL leave, the unused
leave is also carried forward annually. If the balance is forfeited, the
remaining balance is encash and added to the Final Settlement (FNF).
• Paid leaves are not granted for the first 3 months of joining date.
• Paid leave includes paid sick days and paid vacation days.
• Leaves can be taken in half or full-day increments.
• On Half day leave employees should work 4.15 hours excluding breaks.
• If an employee takes leave before and after a holiday, the holiday in between
will also be counted as leave.
• CL Leaves cannot be carried over to the next calendar year.
• Leaves of 2 days or more must be scheduled and approved 1 week in advance.
• Employees must wait for approval of their leave application from the
concerned person. If employees take leave without approval, the leave will be
considered unpaid leave.
• Any changes to scheduled leaves must be communicated to the reporting
manager verbally in advance and via email.
• Leaves of 2 or more Employees from the same team at the same time will not
be approved. In this situation, the Employee who has the extreme urge to take
leave will only be considered.
• Medical or sick leave requires the submission of a doctor's certificate;
otherwise, the leave will be considered unpaid.
• Leaving without prior notification will result in the leave being considered
unpaid.
• The company provide a half-day leave to employees on their birthday.
Working at Home:
• Permanent working from home is not allowed.
• Temporary working from home needs to be scheduled and approved by
the Team Lead and Management at least 2 days prior. After approval,
the working from home schedule must be communicated to all team
members well in advance.
